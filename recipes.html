Grandma's Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 6 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Pie crust (top and bottom)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix apples with sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
  3. Fill crust, top with second crust, and crimp edges.
  4. Bake for 50 minutes until golden brown.
