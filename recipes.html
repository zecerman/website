Shrimp Scampi

    

        

            

                
Ingredients

                
    
                    
  • 1 lb. raw shrimp
    • 
                    
  • 1 cup noodles
    • 
                    
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 
                    
  • minced garlic & lemon juice
    • 
                    
  • salt, pepper, & sesame seeds
    • 
                

                
Instructions

                
    
                    
  1. Heat water to a boil, add noodles and cook until al-dente (~8 mins).
    2. 
                    
  2. Set stove temp to hot and add oil to pan and leave until sizzling.
    3. 
                    
  3. Add garlic, shrimp + lemon juice and cook until red (~4 mins).
    4. 
                    
  4. Remove pan from heat and stir in noodles + remaining ingredients.
    5. 
                

            

            
        

    





    
Stovetop Burgers

    

        

            

                
Ingredients

                
    
                    
  • 1 lb. 80/20 ground beef
    • 
                    
  • white onion, green pepper, minced garlic
    • 
                    
  • 2 eggs, 1/8 cup panko bread crumbs
    • 
                    
  • store bought hamburger seasoning and buns
    • 
                    
  • a drizzle of olive oil
    • 
                

                
Instructions

                
    
                    
  1. Preheat oil filled pan to high.
    2. 
                    
  2. Add onion, garlic, & green pepper to pan, it should be filled.
    3. 
                    
  3. Mold patties out of beef, egg, & bread crumbs in mixing bowl.
    4. 
                    
  4. Make room for patties, add them and cover with cooked vegetables.
    5. 
                    
  5. After cooked halfway (~4 mins) flip patties and cover again.
    6. 
                    
  6. Remove and serve when cooked to medium rare (~4 mins).
    7. 
                

            

            
        

    





    
Protien Pancakes

    

        

            

                
Ingredients

                
    
                    
  • 2 cups Bisquick
    • 
                    
  • 1 cup milk
    • 
                    
  • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 
                    
  • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 
                    
  • 4 eggs
    • 
                    
  • 1 tbsp butter (or bacon grease)
    • 
                

                
Instructions

                
    
                    
  1. Add milk to a large mixing bowl, then add all dry ingredients.
    2. 
                    
  2. While mixing, bring a pan to med-low heat and add butter.
    3. 
                    
  3. Add 4 pancakes of batter to sizzling pan leaving one spot open, cook for 2 min.
    4. 
                    
  4. In the empty spot, add 1 egg's whites to pan and flip a pancake on top of it.
    5. 
                    
  5. Repeat step 4 until all pancakes have been flipped, remove each after 2 min.
    6. 
                

            

            
        

    





    
Perfect Smoothie

    

        

            

                
Ingredients

                
    
                    
  • 6 apples, peeled and sliced
    • 
                    
  • 3/4 cup sugar
    • 
                    
  • 2 tbsp flour
    • 
                    
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • 
                    
  • Pie crust (top and bottom)
    • 
                

                
Instructions

                
    
                    
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. 
                    
  2. Mix apples with sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
    3. 
                    
  3. Fill crust, top with second crust, and crimp edges.
    4. 
                    
  4. Bake for 50 minutes until golden brown.
    5.
